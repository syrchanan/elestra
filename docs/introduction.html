Lore of Elestra

1 Introduction

The layout of this History is both evolving and changing constantly. However, there are many sections which will deign to organize the information, wherever possible.

Clearly, Elestra offers many areas of study, which shall begin with the general history of the land, then move into specifics of peoples, races, and religions.
